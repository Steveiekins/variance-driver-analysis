# Monthly Variance Driver Analysis (Actual vs Budget)

## Problem 
Identify recurring and month-specific drivers of financial variance between actuals and budget.

## Approach
- Normalized and validated raw actuals and budget data
- Aggregated data to monthly account level
- Computed variance and ranked drivers by absolute impact per month
- Synthesized results into executive-level insights

## Outcome
Identified Product Sales revenue underperformance as a recurring structural driver and Marketing expense as a volatile secondary contributor.
